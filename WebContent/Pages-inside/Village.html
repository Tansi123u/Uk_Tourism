Inside the Villages of Uttarakhand

Agoda Village

Agoda Village is the first village that you’ll come across on Dodital Trek.

Bampa Village

Bampa is a village situated in Joshimath Tehsil of district Chamoli of Uttarakhand state.

BhaironGhati

Bhaironghati is a small settlement at the juncture of the Jadh Ganga and Bhagirathi rivers in the mountains of northern India.

Bigul Village

About Bigul Village Bigul is a beautiful hidden destination thatoffers a stunning view of the Himalayas from majestic Nanda Devi.

Kharsali

The untouched village of Kharsali also known as ‘Khushimath’ is cradled with love in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand.

Khurpatal

The emerald blue-green lake of Khurpatal is cradled in the Nainital district of Uttarakhand.

Madkot Village

Madkot is a picturesque hamlet, which is blessed with scenic splendor, as the place is rich in vegetation.

Urgam Village

Urgam Village is a scenic hamlet tucked at an elevation of about 2100 mts Surrounded by snow-clad peaks of the Garhwal Himalayas

Tarikhet Village

Tarikhet is a solitary village located near the sister village of Ranikhet which literally suggests ‘Queen’s Meadow’.

Ukhimath

Ukhimath is the home of Lord Kedarnath and Madhyamaheshwar during winters.
