Nainital City of Lakes

Description

Nainital is a charming hill station located at the foothills of the Kumaon ranges in Uttarakhand. Located close to Dehradun and Delhi, it is the most visited hill station in North India. Nainital is a perfect weekend getaway from Delhi and the nearby places.Nainital experiences a pleasant climate throughout the year, making it a popular hill station for families, couples and even solo travellers.
